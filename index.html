Alissa Stout

Multimedia Specialist

I am a self-motivated individual and one-woman content creation team. My expertise is in efficiently and cost-effectively producing high-quality social media content and training materials. As an in-house media production expert, I manage and complete all levels of the production process, eliminating the need for a large-scale team. I excel at managing limited resources to maximize the quality and functionality of the final product. In addition to creation, I am also skilled at distribution and have successfully managed high-profile social media channels on YouTube and Tiktok, earning millions of views and thousands of followers while presenting our brand in the best possible light and driving substantial traffic to our website.

Though skilled at working independently, I also relish the opportunity to work with other experts. I’ve successfully collaborated with SME’s, creative directors, graphic designers, and animators to produce projects of the highest quality.

One of the joys of my career has been the opportunity to use my passion for video production, filmmaking, and storytelling in my day-to-day work, helping companies to inspire and inform their workforce and connecting customers with the content and products they need. I am an experienced project manager and have overseen hundreds of media projects from conception to completion.
